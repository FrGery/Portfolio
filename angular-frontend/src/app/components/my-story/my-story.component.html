My Story

Where it all started

Back in 1995, my parents generated me with early AI — running on limited hardware but infinite curiosity.

As soon as I gained consciousness, I found myself fascinated by technology.

Tinkering with hardware and software became my hobby, and before long, I was that Tech-Guy everyone called when something beeped, blinked, or broke.

Studies

In High-School, I attended an English preparatory program and graduated with an advanced level in Biology and earned a B2 English language certificate. At that time, I hadn’t yet realized that I wanted to pursue a career in IT.

Later, I studied Hospitality and Economics at Budapest Business School , where I earned my degree in Economics.

After several years of work experience, I eventually found my true path and completed a Full-Stack Developer bootcamp.

Work Experience

From concepts to production: learning to deliver reliable, maintainable features and collaborate effectively.

Coding found me

I love turning ideas into well-structured code – and I’m always exploring better patterns, tools, and practices.
